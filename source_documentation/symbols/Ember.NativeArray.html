Ember.NativeArray
The NativeArray mixin contains the properties needed to to make the native Array support Ember.MutableArray and all of its dependent APIs. Unless you have Ember.EXTEND_PROTOTYPES set to false, this will be applied automatically. Otherwise you can apply the mixin at anytime by calling `Ember.NativeArray.activate`.
